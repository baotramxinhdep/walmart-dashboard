Regular Air is the major contributor to late shipments, especially in Office Supplies.
Strategies: Optimize Regular Air Logistics
Technology is the primary profit engine but faces risks from high shipping costs on specific orders.
High discount rates (over 15%) are causing significant losses, especially within the Furniture category.
Strategies: Implement discount caps, optimize bulky item logistics, and scale high-performing Technology products.
Core Portfolio: Technology dominates as the 'Cash Cow' with high market share and profit, while Office Supplies acts as a 'Star' with strong growth and increasing share.
The 'Dogs' Risk:Several Furniture and small Office Supplies segments show negative growth and low share, draining resources without providing returns.
Strategy: Harvest profits from Technology to fund Office Supplies' expansion, while aggressively phasing out or restructuring underperforming Furniture lines.
Margin Danger:High discounts (>20%) crush margins, especially for Tables (severe loss).
Sweet Spot: Profitability is strongest and most stable at the 0-10% discount range.
Strategy: Eliminate discounts over 10% for Furniture/Tables and maintain the 0-5% range for high-margin Office Supplies to maximize overall bottom-line health.
Lợi nhuận theo thành phố: Cần tập trung cải thiện hiệu quả tại các thành phố thuộc Danger Zone.
Corporatee High Profit & efficiency. --> Maintain high service levels and use as a model for other segments.
Consumer: High Sales but driven by high Discounts and Shipping costs. --> Reduce discounts and optimize logistics to protect margins.
Small Business & Home Office: High Order Quantity/On-Time rate but low Profit. --> Up-sell high-margin products and bundle items to offset shipping costs.
Bản đồ Mỹ: Khu vực Miền Tây có mức chi phí vận chuyển trung bình (Avg Shipping Cost) cao nhất.
Prioritize Home Office: Highest median CLV makes it the most reliable segment for ROI.
Retain VIPs: Deploy exclusive loyalty programs for high-CLV outliers in the Consumer and Corporate segments.
Cost-Effective Retention: Use automated, low-cost remarketing for 'Low-Value' clusters to improve their lifetime value without overspending.
